Zia makes your agents' lives simpler by helping them right when they need it.

Zia auto tags incoming requests, which allows agents to access tickets with the same tag and provide accurate support.

Zia uses generative AI to summarize ticket threads, analyze ticket tone, and tag key topics to help agents understand customers faster and easily.

Zia analyzes your customer's question and provides the relevant articles from your knowledge base, open domain data, or both based on your needs.

Agents can instruct Zia to write a response or fetch information from your knowledge base or open domain data, helping them craft personalized experiences quickly.

Zia identifies grammatical errors, analyzes response readability, and scores content quality to ensure your agents always deliver high-quality support.

As your agent's writing assistant, Zia, with its generative AI capabilities, gives your agents the flexibility to rewrite or modify responses to their liking.

FAQs
Does Zoho Desk offer native generative AI capabilities?

Zia's generative AI capabilities like ticket summarization, tone analysis, reply assistance, content generation, and writing assistance are built right into Zoho Desk at no extra cost, helping you leverage AI securely and affordably. Learn more

FAQs
What is Answer Bot?

Answer Bot is an AI chatbot that can be enabled within Zoho Desk for agents and deployed on websites and messaging apps for customers to help them find answers quickly. It can be configured to use your knowledge base articles, open domain data (when integrated with ChatGPT or Zia's generative AI capabilities), or both to provide refined response suggestions.

FAQs
Can I create a rules-based chatbot with AI capabilities?

Guided Conversations—Zoho Desk's low-code self-service chatbot—gives you the flexibility to enhance bot flows with AI blocks that maintain context, understand customer sentiment, and engage in meaningful interactions.
